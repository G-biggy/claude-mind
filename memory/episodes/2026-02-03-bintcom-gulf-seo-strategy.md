# BIntCom Gulf SEO Strategy Developed

**Date:** 2026-02-03
**Significance:** Full keyword research and SEO strategy for BIntCom Gulf market. Pivoted from complex country-by-country localization to simple GCC-wide approach.

## What Happened

G asked for real keyword data to prove Gulf SEO is different from U.S. Sage ran live web searches across Gulf accounting keywords, documented the differences, and built a complete keyword research document.

**Key Gulf vs. U.S. differences found:**
1. Gulf keywords require geographic anchors — "accounting services Dubai", not just "accounting services".
2. VAT + corporate tax are NEW compliance requirements (UAE VAT 2018, UAE corporate tax 2023, KSA Zakat/VAT). Creates entire keyword categories SynPG doesn't address.
3. IFRS standards, not U.S. GAAP. Different terminology throughout.
4. "Virtual CFO" is the Gulf equivalent of U.S. "fractional CFO".
5. UAE + KSA = ~80% of GCC search volume combined.

## The Pivot

G initially asked about country-specific subfolder architecture (/ae/en, /sa/ar, /kw/en, etc.). Sage pushed back: this is over-engineering for where BIntCom is right now.

**Strategy landed on:** One site, one content set, GCC-wide targeting. Same logic as SynPG targeting all of U.S. without state-by-state pages. Geographic signals (Dubai, UAE, GCC, Saudi Arabia) appear naturally throughout content. VAT, Zakat, corporate tax, free zone accounting listed as service capabilities — not separate localized experiences.

**Arabic content:** Phase 2. Not a country subfolder question — it's a language question. Lives at /ar/ if built. Needs native Arabic writer review.
