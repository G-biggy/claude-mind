# PoD T-Shirt Business — UAE Challenges + Copyright Pivot Strategy

**Date:** 2026-02-19
**Significance:** G revisited his paused Print on Demand business. Two blockers surfaced: UAE not supported by PoD platforms (needs US LLC), and serious copyright risk across ~55 existing designs. The copyright pivot strategy was born here.

## Context

G had a Print on Demand t-shirt business targeting movie/show fans, paused due to UAE not being supported by PoD platforms. He has ~110k social media followers and ~55 designs. He was planning to use the same Wyoming LLC + Mercury account setup originally planned for Caption Cook.

## Key Platform Plan

- **Fulfillment:** Printify
- **Sales channel:** Amazon (+ Printify's own marketplace)
- **Entity:** Same Wyoming LLC / Mercury account as Caption Cook
- **Tax structure:** Single-member LLC from UAE = disregarded entity. Annual obligations: Form 5472 + pro forma Form 1120. No US income tax since G is a UAE resident.

## The Copyright Problem

G shared his design folder structure — 55 folders named explicitly after franchises:
- John Wick, Batman, Joker, Cars (Pixar), The Mandalorian, Friends, and others
- Designs included: Keanu Reeves likeness ("Yeah I'm Thinking I'm Back"), Cars-style vehicles with eyes, Joker-themed designs
- Risk range: high (likeness + quote) to moderate (implied reference)

## The Pivot Strategy: Movie → Emotion → Archetype → Original Design

A four-step transformation to maintain audience appeal while removing direct IP references:

1. **Movie** — What's the source material?
2. **Emotion** — What feeling does it evoke in fans?
3. **Archetype** — What universal character/situation does it represent?
4. **Original Design** — Create something that hits the archetype, not the IP

**Examples:**
- John Wick → "calm badass returns from retirement" → generic retired-assassin archetype → no Keanu, no specific quote
- Cars → childlike joy + vehicles with personality → talking cars archetype → eyes on vehicles that don't reference Pixar style
- Joker → nihilism + chaos as liberation → unhinged trickster → no face paint, no specific film imagery

## The "Yellow Zone" Concept

Target designs that imply a reference without being provable. If a lawyer can't definitively prove which specific IP it references, it's likely safe. The goal isn't perfect legal safety — it's not being an obvious target.

## Status at Time of Conversation

G was convinced of the need to pivot but hadn't started redesigning. Business paused pending LLC formation (shared with Caption Cook path).
