# SynPG Company Overview PDF Designed

**Date:** 2026-02-27
**Significance:** Client wanted a brochure to share with clients via email. Print brochure is wrong deliverable for that — pivoted to a company overview PDF.

## What Happened

Client originally asked for a brochure. G delivered a print version, then realized they actually wanted something to send by email. Print brochures are constrained (fold constraints, limited space). A multi-page PDF overview is the correct deliverable for B2B email sharing.

Sage framed the positioning and wrote all content from two source documents: the SynPG SEO content doc + their existing brochure PDF (which had extra claims not on the website: "12 countries", "reduce costs up to 60%", four-pillar service framing, "eliminate turnover risk").

**Document structure (6 spreads):**
1. Cover
2. Services (organized in groups, not full 9-item list)
3. Why SynPG (outcome-focused: 60% cost reduction, turnover elimination, decision-ready financials, scalability)
4. Proven Results ($180M portfolio, Top 225 company, outcomes list)
5. Getting Started (3-step process: Consultation → Onboarding → Start Working)
6. Contact CTA

**Two versions delivered:**
- With chapter dividers: 19 pages
- Without chapter dividers: 12 pages

Both sent to Amr and Danielle with email drafted by Sage. Awaiting client feedback on direction.

**Design note:** This same design system (layout, visual language) is the presentation deck template and corporate identity foundation.
