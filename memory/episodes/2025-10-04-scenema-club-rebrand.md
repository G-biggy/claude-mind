# Scenema Club Rebrand Planning

**Date:** 2025-10-04
**Significance:** Pre-project history — first Claude conversation found in archives. Reveals G's film culture side project.

## Context

Two conversations from October 4, 2025 surfaced during memory mining:
1. `amman cinemas vs scenema club`
2. `Scenema club transition strategy`

These predate the claude-mind project (which started Feb 10, 2026) and contain important biographical context.

## What Happened

G was assessing three old cinema-related domains he'd held for years (ammancinema.com, ammancinemas.com, jordancinema.com). As the conversation developed, the full picture emerged:

- He had built Amman Cinemas into the go-to Jordan movie listings site
- COVID destroyed the use case; site went dormant but social accounts kept going
- He had built ~136k followers across platforms around film culture content
- He decided to rebrand globally as **Scenema Club** instead of maintaining a Jordan-specific brand

## Key Decisions Made

- Let all Amman/Jordan domains expire rather than renew
- Secure `scenema.club` and `scenemaclub.com`
- Change all social handles to @scenemaclub
- Keep it as a social hub (simple HTML landing page) until there's a real project
- Transition plan was mapped out in Notion with a 50-task database across 3 phases

## State During This Conversation

- G was job hunting / unemployed
- Working from Android phone, often late nights
- Had limited time for content creation (last post was ~2 months prior)
- Acknowledged monetization potential but wasn't the immediate focus
