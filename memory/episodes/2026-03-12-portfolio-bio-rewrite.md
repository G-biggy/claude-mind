# Portfolio Bio Rewrite for ghayyath.com

## What Happened
G wanted to update the bio on ghayyath.com to better reflect the full spectrum of what he does — not just design, but vibe coding and shipping products. Reviewed the live portfolio site and identified that the current presentation (title + one-liner + link per project) doesn't communicate that G both designs AND builds his products.

Also drafted a Caption Cook description for the portfolio:
> Turn any recipe from social media or food blogs into clear ingredients, instructions, and macros. Drop in a screenshot, paste text, or share a link — get protein, carbs, and fat per serving instantly.

## Decisions Made
- **New bio line added**: "Vibe coder on the loose 🏎️" — stands on its own, not merged into the designer line
- **"AI-curious" upgraded to "AI-native"** — reflects actual depth (building with Claude API, not just exploring)
- **"Building stuff" changed to "Shipping products"** — concrete, broader than "apps", covers tools/extensions too
- **Emoji swap**: 🎧 (headphones, cliché) → 🏎️ (racing car, matches "on the loose" energy, unexpected in tech bio)
- **Reordered lines**: AI stuff moved before design systems for better narrative flow

### Final bio:
> Movie buff behind Amman Cinemas 🎬 | Seasoned product/UX/UI designer breaking UX rules 🧪 | Vibe coder on the loose 🏎️ | AI-native 🤖 | Design system enthusiast 📐 | Shipping products with curiosity 💡, AI ✨, coffee ☕ and mild chaos 🧨
