## Temenos Reassessment

The Temenos role actually doesn't need someone who:
1. Can facilitate external customer workshops (though he could learn)
2. Has formal design thinking certification (nice-to-have)
3. Is a specialist researcher (he's not)

It needs someone who:
1. Can do research and translate insights ✅ (he's proven this)
2. Can facilitate strategy + ideation ✅ (his case study shows this)
3. Can mentor teams on design methodology ✅ (he's done this)
4. Can lead vision/strategy work ✅ (this is his main mode)

**Key insight:** The gap wasn't as big as initially assessed. The real question is: Does G want a research-first, methodology-heavy, external-facilitation role? Or does he prefer a product-first, execution-focused, builder role?

That's the *real* fit question, not capability.
