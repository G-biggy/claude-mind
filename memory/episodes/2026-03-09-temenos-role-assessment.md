# Temenos Lead Product Designer Role Assessment

## What Happened
G shared the Temenos "Lead Product Designer - Design Partner Program" JD (London/Orlando/Dubai, Hybrid, posted today). Initial enthusiasm was tempered by honest self-assessment.

**Role DNA:**
- Focus on Design Thinking methodology + facilitation
- External customer co-design workshops (not internal requirement gathering)
- Research strategist first, executor second
- Design thinking certification valued (IDEO, Stanford Design School, Design Council)
- Service design and journey mapping emphasis
- Mentoring on design thinking frameworks

**G's Actual Strengths:**
- Pragmatic product design leader (20 years, 8 years logistics platforms)
- Conducted user interviews + business feedback collection (not formal research methodology)
- Facilitated internal stakeholder requirement translation (not external customer co-design)
- Builder/executor with business results focus
- Team mentorship and design system architecture (but team executed the system)
- Design ops learned by doing, not formalized
- Design thinking was "so-so" - no documentation, driven by speed/business pressure

## Decision Made
**SKIP THIS ROLE.**

The gap is real and unbridgeable without fabrication:
- G does not do formal design thinking workshops with customers
- G is not trained/certified in design thinking methodology
- G's research is interview-based + feedback, not formal research practice
- Attempting to retrofit would create inauthenticity that would be caught in interviews

G's principle: "I don't like lying, coz I will get caught eventually and that hurts my credibility more than anything."

This principle is **non-negotiable and correct**. Reframing wouldn't help here.

## What G Actually Should Target
Roles that value:
- **Product design leadership** at fintech scale-ups (builders, not facilitators)
- **Design Director** at B2B SaaS (systems thinking + team leadership)
- **Head of Product Design** mid-stage scaling (what he's already done)
- **Principal Product Designer** at results-focused companies

NOT:
- Research-forward strategy roles
- Service design specialist roles
- Design thinking facilitation roles

## System Changes
- Marked Temenos as "assessed, not a fit" in job-search-history
- Reinforced approach: authentic self-assessment > retrofitting CVs
- Clarified target persona for future applications

## Key Insight
The credibility principle is worth protecting. Better to skip 10 roles than to land one, struggle in interviews, and damage professional reputation. G understands this.
