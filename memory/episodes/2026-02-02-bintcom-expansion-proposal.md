# BIntCom Expansion + SynPG Additional Services Proposal

**Date:** 2026-02-02
**Significance:** SynPG website delivered. Client (Amr + Danielle) returned with a scope expansion covering both BIntCom (sister company) and additional SynPG services.

## What Happened

SynPG website was fully approved and handed off. Client came back with new asks:

**BIntCom services needed:**
- Logo: same Growth concept, same orange palette, brand connection between companies. JOD 100 (discounted from SynPG's JOD 200 since concept exists).
- Website design: Dynamic option (vs SynPG's Clean). Home page already approved. Remaining pages + responsive. JOD 300 (75% of original — no concept exploration needed).
- SEO localization for Gulf/GCC market: JOD 70.
- No development or integration — tech team handles everything.

**SynPG additional services:**
- Corporate identity package: brochure + business card + letterhead. JOD 200 bundled.

## Key Decisions

- One unified proposal document (same decision-makers, same budget). Path: ~/VS-workspace/SynPG/_SynPG+BIntCon Proposal.pdf
- Brochure: offer 2 layout concepts. Business card and letterhead follow chosen direction — no need to offer multiple options for standardized pieces.
- Arabic SEO added as optional Phase 2 (JOD 50) requiring native writer review.
- BIntCom logo is JOD 100, not JOD 200, because the creative concept is already established — fair discount since G doesn't redo the exploration phase.
