# Relocation

## What
G conducted deep, systematic relocation research across 20+ countries from late 2024 through mid-2025, with job-search-driven relocation continuing into Feb 2026. This was not casual browsing — he evaluated visa pathways, citizenship timelines, cost of living, schools for his kids, and inclusivity for his wife (covered Muslim woman).

## Key Criteria G Uses for Country Evaluation
- Pathway to citizenship (speed matters)
- Passport strength
- Cost of living for family of 5 (soon to be 5 — new baby planned)
- British/IGCSE schools for son (14, Year 10) and daughter (10, on the spectrum, lactose+gluten sensitive, English-only speaker)
- Low discrimination/racism (wife wears hijab)
- Ability to work remotely or find employment
- Healthcare quality (especially for daughter's needs)
- Quality of life and safety
