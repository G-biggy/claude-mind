## SEO Strategy Notes

- SynPG targets U.S. as one market (no state-by-state pages). BIntCom mirrors this: GCC as one market, no country subfolders.
- Gulf keywords require geographic anchors — standalone terms don't work like U.S.
- Three forces shaping Gulf keywords: (1) VAT + corporate tax are new/recent compliance needs, (2) geography modifiers required, (3) IFRS vs U.S. GAAP creates different terminology.
- "virtual CFO" is the Gulf term equivalent to U.S. "fractional CFO".
- SynPG SEO content has H1 + paragraph hero on every page including blog list and blog post templates.
