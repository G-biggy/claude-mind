**Problem:** Operations staff were spending 3.2 hours/day on manual workarounds across 3 platforms. 73% of daily tasks required switching between 2–3 systems. 3-week onboarding for new staff.

**What G Did:**
- Conducted 25+ user interviews and extensive field research
- Led 6 weeks of research + co-design sessions with operations teams
- Designed the complete UX strategy and design system
- Managed 3 designers, 4 engineers, 2 QA (per one version)

**Results:**

- 85% reduction in manual work
- Onboarding time: 1 week → 2 days (some versions say 3 weeks → 4 days)
- 95% user adoption within 1 month
