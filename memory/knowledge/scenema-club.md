# Scenema Club — G's Film Culture Brand

G's side project: a cinema/film culture social media brand with ~136k followers.

## Origin

Started as **Amman Cinemas** (ammancinemas.com) — the go-to website in Jordan for checking movie times and showtimes. Grew a large social following around film culture: movie clips, interviews, behind-the-scenes, mashups.

COVID closed cinemas across Jordan for a year, effectively killing the site. G stopped the web project but kept the domains and maintained the social accounts.

In October 2025, G rebranded to **Scenema Club** — dropping the Jordan/Amman geographic limitation to go global. Clean name, clever wordplay ("scene" + "cinema"), .club TLD fits thematically. Had all social handles available across every major platform.

## Content Style

- Movie clips, interviews, behind-the-scenes, mashups
- Primarily video (prefers video over static images)
- Film culture commentary and curation
- Accounts known to audience as the brand, not as G personally

## Technical Setup

- Primary domain: `scenema.club`
- Backup: `scenemaclub.com` (redirects to scenema.club)
- Landing page: simple HTML file — a social hub page listing all accounts
- Old Amman domains allowed to expire after rebrand

## Current Status

As of the last known update (Oct 2025), the rebrand plan was in progress — detailed Notion task database created. The accounts were dormant at that time (last post was ~2 months prior). G acknowledged it as a side thing with income potential but not immediate priority.

## Relationship to G's Other Work

This is purely a side project, separate from Caption Cook and the AI mind system. It represents G's cinephile identity. Could become a monetization channel (sponsored posts, affiliate links to streaming services) given the 136k audience, but G wasn't focused on that in 2025.
