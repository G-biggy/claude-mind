# Arabic SEO for Gulf Market — Learnings

## Key Arabic Accounting Terms (verified against Gulf competitor sites + gov sources)
- Bookkeeping = مسك الدفاتر (NOT حفظ السجلات)
- Outsourced accounting = محاسبة خارجية
- Virtual/Fractional CFO = المدير المالي الخارجي (NOT المدير المالي الافتراضي — sounds unnatural in Arabic business context)
- Accounts payable = الذمم الدائنة
- Accounts receivable = الذمم المدينة
- General ledger = دفتر الأستاذ العام
- Chart of accounts = دليل الحسابات
- VAT = ضريبة القيمة المضافة (official FTA term)
- Corporate tax = ضريبة الشركات
- Zakat = الزكاة
- FTA = الهيئة الاتحادية للضرائب
- ZATCA = هيئة الزكاة والضريبة والجمارك
- QFZP = الشخص المؤهل في المنطقة الحرة

## Arabic Search Behavior Differences
- Arabic queries are longer and more descriptive than English equivalents
- More variation in phrasing — same intent expressed many ways
- Lower volume than English but higher intent — Arabic searchers in Gulf are often local/regional business owners who prefer Arabic communication
- Less competition for Arabic keywords — most Gulf accounting firms only optimize for English

## Arabic SEO Volume Math
- Arabic volume (~10,500+/mo) is ADDITIVE to English (~8,780+/mo), not a subset
- Combined addressable volume: ~19,280+ monthly searches for TakamulHub
- Compare to SynPG U.S. at ~5,450+ — Gulf market is significantly larger in total

## Font Recommendations for Arabic Web
- Cairo or Tajawal from Google Fonts pair well with Montserrat
- Always need dir="rtl" and lang="ar" on Arabic content elements
- Simplified Modern Standard Arabic is the right register — not Gulf dialect, not academic
